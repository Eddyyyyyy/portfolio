Project Overview.

The transition from high school to college is not easy. Balancing a heavier workload while keeping up with exercise, friends, and work make it extremely overwhelming for many students. With all this stress, it is not surprising that students face mental health issues.

This problem inspired my team to create a time-management app to lift some responsibilities off students' shoulders, so they can focus on enjoying the college experience.

I collaborated with my teammates on user research, revised the wireframes to be consistent, and was responsible for creating the whole prototype along with its animations.

“How might we reduce the stresses of college life on freshmen?”
