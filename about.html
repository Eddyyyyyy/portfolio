Bio

A bit about me!

Hello there, I'm sure you know my name already! I'm a Junior majoring in Informatics (HCI) at the University of Washington in Seattle.

I am also minoring in Art History. Art History has taught me the importance of function versus form. By applying my awareness of Art and Culture, I design simple and efficient solutions that make people happy.

When I am not designing, I take part in cloud photography! Click this magical link to learn more about me!

Why UX?

Growing up, I had a fascination with web experiences.

As an avid internet user since middle school, I would say to myself again and again: "if only they had changed this element instead, my experience would be so much better!" Initially, I ignored my UX thoughts and went along with my day.

However, it was in my senior year of high school when my passion for UX ignited. I began falling in love with the writing process, particularly the peer review and feedback stages. I felt good when I improved on my original content after processing suggestions from others.

It wasn't long before I realized that as a UX Designer, I could fulfill my early desires of improving digital experiences and getting feedback from others about the work that I'm doing!
