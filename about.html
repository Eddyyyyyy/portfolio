Bio

A bit about me!

Hello there, I'm sure you know my name already! I'm a Senior majoring in Informatics (HCI) at the University of Washington in Seattle.

I am also minoring in Art History. Art History has taught me the importance of function versus form. By applying my awareness of Art and Culture, I design simple and efficient solutions that make people happy.

When I am not designing, I take part in cloud photography! Click this magical link to learn more about me!


Why UX?

Growing up, I had a fascination with web experiences.

As a dedicated internet user since middle school, I often thought of ways to improve website user flows to enhance the overall user experience. At first, I disregarded these thoughts and continued with my daily routines. However, my passion for UX design ignited during my senior year of high school.

I discovered I enjoyed the writing process, particularly the peer review and feedback stages. It was satisfying to incorporate suggestions from others to improve my original content. As I delved deeper into UX design, I realized I could fulfill my desire to enhance digital experiences and receive constructive feedback by pursuing a career in this field.

What am I doing currently?

Some of my current work includes leading my team at Tunnel_vzn, taking on client projects, and collaborating with engineers on new business ideas. On top of everything, I enjoy making improvements to my website portfolio and enhancing my UI design skills.
