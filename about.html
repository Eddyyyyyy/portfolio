A Bit About Me

Hey there, my name is Eddy, and I am a Junior majoring in Informatics(HCI) at the University of Washington in Seattle.

I am also minoring in Art History. Art History has taught me the importance of function versus form. By applying my awareness in Art and Culture, I design simple and efficient solutions that make people happy.

When I am not designing, I take part in cloud photography! Click this magical link to learn more about me!

Why UX?

I had a technology-loathing mindset during elementary school because of the software they had used for assessments. The UX was so terrible that it led my classmates to accidentally submit unfinished exams.

Fast forward to today, as a designer, I seek to alleviate the frustration of using technology. By improving tech-usability, people can see tech as a helpful tool rather than an inconvenience.
