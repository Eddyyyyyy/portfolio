A bit about myself.

My Values.

Respect others & their ideas

Each one of us has different experiences, perspectives, and ideas. Unfortunately, we are not able to read each other's minds to know what they are thinking. Therefore, we have to make our views known to others through our actions, either through writing or speaking. Every person and their ideas have the right to be heard.

Be human

As humans, we often make mistakes. However, that is ok. By making mistakes, we remind ourselves not to make that mistake again. To do that, we reflect on why it occurred and what steps to take the next time it happens.

No place for ego

I am currently a college student; I do not know everything there is about UX design. That will be true even after I graduate. Since UX design is a rapidly evolving field, there will always be room for growth. Keep an open mind, ask questions, don't be afraid to look "bad" in front of others.
