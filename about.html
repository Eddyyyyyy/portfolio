Bio

A bit about me!

Hello there, I'm sure you know my name already! I'm a Senior in Informatics (HCI) at the University of Washington in Seattle.

I am a product designer who thrives in fast-paced, collaborative teams through innovation and storytelling to stay ahead of design and business demands. I have delivered tangible results at companies in fintech, education, consulting, energy, mental health, and healthcare.

Why UX?

Before beginning my User Experience career, I was a writer.

I enjoyed writing stories and essays because I loved the process, which included brainstorming, drafting, getting feedback, and iterating. My love of writing led me to Product Design, where I continued to fulfill my enjoyment of improving something through a process, being creative, and telling stories while mixing in the problem-solving and business aspects.
