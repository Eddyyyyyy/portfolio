Zivolve By Ziggurat

  • Interaction Designer
  • Internship
  • Mobile App
  • NDA

Overview

I interned at Ziggurat and worked on their Zivolve AI investment app. I learned on the fly and communicated to overcome challenges.

www.zivolve.com

Tools

Figma

Duration

May - Jul 2022 (3 Months)

Why Zivolve?

Hedge funds get rich, while 95% of market traders lose $$$.

Due to experience and technology gaps, the playing field heavily favors hedge funds and corporations over individuals. Zivolve levels the playing field by using AI to suggest decisions based on market trends.

The Process

Every design task followed a 6-stage process.

The Challenge

Rapid concept comprehension.

On top of learning essential investment fundamentals and concepts on the fly, I applied design thinking, followed design system guidelines, and ensured the best UI practices for my work. When I had knowledge gaps, I asked for clarification. Not doing so would increase the likelihood of a faulty design.

Impact

Trusted in designing essential features.

My co-intern and I designed many features essential to the product’s success. Most screens are still in the agile development process and will be live in the upcoming 5 product versions.

Reflections

Simplifying complexities is significant in UX.

Before starting my internship, I had limited knowledge of asset trading. However, I understood critical concepts by conducting research and receiving guidance from the team.

It made me realize that understanding complexities is something few people can fit into their schedules. Making the complex understandable is critical in designing people-focused interfaces.
