What is Tunnel_vzn?

Tunnel_vzn is a story-telling website that comforts demoralized University of Washington (UW) Students. The website consists of case studies that provide UW Students an objective glimpse into social obstacles they and other students face daily. By providing new perspectives through story-telling, UW Students will understand that they are not alone in their struggles and will feel empowered to walk forward in life.
