I realized that I needed to make improvements to my design skills. So, I embarked on a journey to examine and create good UI!

Dec 2022 - Present

12-19-2022

A sparkling water shopping app.

I wanted to experiment with a frost gradient background, and during this phase in my life, I was big into sparkling water because it was marginally better than sugary sodas. I sought to capture the feeling refreshment.

12-21-2022

A calculator.

This interface displays recent operations and calculation history. I figured the calculator would be incorporated into a mobile operating system, allowing appearance and functionality changes through their device.

12-24-2022

A landing page for a caffè.

As an avid coffee drinker, I've always dreamed of owning a caffè in the future. So, I decided to make a landing page for my future establishment. I will flush out the rest of the landing page when more ideas come to mind.

12-26-2022

A giftcard management app.

Many times, I have received many different gift cards. From spending to account balances, I created a manager application to track them all. I paid close attention to the shopping breakdowns and the display of numbers.

12-27-2022

A health reccommendation application.

PocketDoc, lets people receive health recommendations based on daily check-ups, medical records, and health assessments. This UI is a revamped version of a course project I worked on.

12-30-2022

A music playlist sharing platform.

A vibrant way to explore new music and learn about other people's tastes, this application retains only the critical UI needed for a music application to function. Simple, yet effective.

01-18-2023

A team management tool sidebar.

Looking to improve my dashboard design skills, I started by creating the variants of the sidebar menu. The completion of these variants ignited my motivation to pursue a complete dashboard.

02-23-2022

A team management dashboard.

After wireframing and analyzing inspirational pieces, I formulated a hi-fi dashboard UI. Throughout the design, I paid close attention to the spacing, sizing, and arrangement of elements and text.
