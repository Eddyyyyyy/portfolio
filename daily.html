Overview

Daily UI consists of 100 daily design challenges. Giving myself an hour to craft something on Figma, I use these challenges as an opportunity for experimentation and self-expression. Here are some of my favorite screens so far!


Day #1: Sign Up

I designed a pop-up page that promotes a candy basket giveaway. After leaving their email, users would get a follow-up email prompting them for shipping details.

Day #2: Credit Card Checkout

I designed a check-out experience for an imaginary company that sells overpriced cardboard boxes. I went for a minimalistic but welcoming experience.

Day #4: Calculator

I envisioned a user input written out similarly to math done manually and on paper. I experimented with neumorphism for the buttons.

Day #7: Settings

I felt innovative and wanted to design something outside of the box. I created the settings for an imaginary illustration application. The user can write the yes/no responses with their stylus.

Day #21: Home Monitoring Dashboard

I have no experience with home monitoring, so I took inspiration from the internet and learned the functionalities that a dashboard may have. Users can manage temperature, lighting, and alerts. Users can also listen to music and view a live cam.
